I am Joe Guaraglia, a tattoo artist dedicated to turning skin into canvas. I specialize in the striking depth of black and grey realism and the timeless, flowing narrative of traditional and modern Japanese large-scale work.

For me, tattooing is a collaboration. Whether capturing the raw emotion of a realistic portrait or the powerful flow of an Irezumi backpiece, we take the time to refine the vision until it perfectly aligns with your story.

You can initiate a project by sharing your concept via the contact section below or through Instagram. Tell me about your placement, desired style (Realism or Japanese), and the vision you have. I review each concept personally to ensure we are a perfect fit creatively.

To maintain the focus required for intricate realism and large-scale tattooing, I work strictly by appointment. If an unexpected opening arises in the studio, I will announce it immediately on Instagram.

You are welcome to bring one guest. I intentionally cultivate a quiet, focused, and calm atmosphere in the studio—this space is designed for patience, comfort, and deep focus during intense sessions.

Arrive well-rested, fully hydrated, and having eaten a solid meal. Wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows easy, unrestricted access to the area we are tattooing. A prepared mind and body make a profound difference during a long session.
